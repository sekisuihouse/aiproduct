# 90 Day Execution

## Objective

Design a repeatable first wedge: proposal and handoff automation for built-world businesses.

## Month 1

- 10 interviews
- 3 design partners
- 1 demoable MVP scope
- 1 outbound campaign
- 1 sales deck

## Month 2

- First pilot onboarded
- Usage feedback loop live
- Pricing test running
- Follow-up sequence active

## Month 3

- Pilot results measured
- Case study draft
- Referral asks sent
- Roadmap updated from evidence

## Exit Criteria

- Clear pain
- Repeat use
- One buyer willing to pay
- One internal workflow repeatedly improved
