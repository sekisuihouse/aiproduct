# Brand Narrative

## Positioning

HomeFlow AI is not a chatbot. It is the operating layer for high-complexity service businesses in the built world.

## Narrative

The built world runs on documents, calls, handoffs, and hidden context.
AI can now turn that hidden context into an operating system.

## Message Pillars

- Reduce manual work
- Preserve institutional knowledge
- Improve customer response speed
- Standardize quality
- Create compounding workflow data
