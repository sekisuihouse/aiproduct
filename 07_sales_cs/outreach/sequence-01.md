# Sequence 01

## Email 1

- Use one painful workflow in one sentence.
- Mention the exact handoff or proposal work that disappears.

## Email 2

- Short follow-up.
- Add one concrete time saver.

## Email 3

- Offer a workflow audit.
- Include one example role or department.

## Email 4

- Close politely.
- Leave the door open for a later workflow review.
