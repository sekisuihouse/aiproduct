# Campaign 002

## Audience

- COO
- Operations lead
- Office manager

## Promise

Reduce hidden coordination work that slows projects and leaks context.

## CTA

15 minute workflow audit

## Angle

- fewer back-and-forth messages
- fewer missing details
- faster handoffs between teams

## Why this exists

- Operations teams feel the pain before executives do.
- This campaign tests whether the buyer cares more about speed or consistency.

## Proof to look for

- repeated correction loops
- missing approval context
- handoff notes stored in many places
