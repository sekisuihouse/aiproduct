# Lead Source Map

## Current sources

- Official contact pages of architecture and design firms
- Business development emails published on company websites
- Region-specific office contact pages
- General inquiry inboxes that route to partnerships or new business

## Priority segments

- Architecture studios with international offices
- General contractors with digital transformation pain
- Design-build firms with proposal-heavy workflows
- Engineering consultancies with BIM and handoff overhead

## Harvest rules

- Only use public, company-published contact channels
- Record source URL next to each lead
- Prefer one role per company until the message proves itself
- Do not duplicate companies across campaigns unless the audience changes materially

## Scaling plan

1. Seed the list with 25 high-fit leads.
2. Expand to 100 by adding adjacent built-environment firms.
3. Expand to 250 by adding regional offices and different business lines only when needed.
4. Expand to 500 by adding adjacent workflows: property, facilities, interiors, and engineering services.
