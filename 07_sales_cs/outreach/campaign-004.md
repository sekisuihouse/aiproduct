# Campaign 004

## Audience

- Design director
- Project lead
- Architect

## Promise

Make proposal and coordination work less chaotic without changing the design process.

## CTA

Review one active workflow

## Angle

- keep creative work intact
- reduce admin load
- preserve context through project handoffs

## Why this exists

- Architects reject vague AI promises.
- This campaign isolates the workflow around the work, not the design itself.

## Proof to look for

- long proposal cycles
- repeated context gathering
- staff spending time formatting instead of thinking
