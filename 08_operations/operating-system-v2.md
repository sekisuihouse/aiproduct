# Operating System v2

## Core Rule

No work without a customer, metric, and decision owner.

## Company Loop

1. Observe customer pain
2. Record evidence
3. Decide one wedge
4. Sell the wedge
5. Deliver the wedge
6. Measure outcome
7. Expand or kill

## Required Fields For Every Initiative

- owner
- goal
- customer segment
- metric
- deadline
- kill condition
- evidence link

## Weekly Execution Stack

- Monday: sales and customer evidence
- Tuesday: product decisions
- Wednesday: engineering and delivery
- Thursday: growth and partnership
- Friday: metrics and reset

## Stop Doing List

- Building generic features without buyers
- Writing long strategy without evidence
- Keeping private operating data in public files
- Starting new campaigns without closing old loops
